Neven Axel

Graphic Design Student

The Axel is a very interesting creature. Some people say this creature can’t exist. But I can tell you, I saw him and studied its behavior. The Axel passes most of its time learning and designing new things. This creature is really curious. One night I saw it whole night on the computer doing tutorials about learning new photoshop skills. I don’t get why people don’t know much about the Axel. It’s an interesting and friendly creature. It moves a lot, I’ve seen it in Russia, Spain, Belgium, United Kingdom and Poland. The Axel seems to be very interested in differents cultures.
